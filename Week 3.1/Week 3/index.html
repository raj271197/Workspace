Children's Lessons

Children's full day lessons meet from 10am through 3:30pm and include lift access, rentals (ski or snowboard), and lunch. All children (ages 6-12) are welcome regardless of ability or prior experience.

Terrain Park Lessons

Our terrain park lessons meet from 12:30pm to 4pm and include lift access, rentals, and an afternoon snack. Must be 13 years old.

Adult Lessons

Adult full day lessons from 9am through 3pm include lift access, rentals, and lunch. Adults are divided by ability and experience.

Cost

All of our lessons are $100/day. Prepay for three full days and get a free season pass.
